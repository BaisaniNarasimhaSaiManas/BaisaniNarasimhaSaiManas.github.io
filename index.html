ABOUT.

I'm Manas Baisani, a B.Tech ECE student at Lovely Professional University with a dual focus on VLSI design and Embedded Systems.

In VLSI, I design and verify digital hardware at RTL level — building processors, ALUs and control units using Verilog HDL, simulated in Xilinx Vivado and Cadence NC-Sim.

On the embedded side, I write firmware for ARM Cortex STM32 and ESP32, implement real-time protocols (UART, SPI, I2C), and build complete IoT systems.

Beyond engineering — I hold a Japanese Language (N5 Level) certification from LPU's CFL and placed first runner-up in PROJECT-O-MANIA.
